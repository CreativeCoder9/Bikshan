Our Story

Mr Arabinda Ghosh commenced the business of providing pathology services in the year 1998 at the heart of Purba Burdhaman, Kalna. At that time, local patients had to go all the way to Kolkata even for basic medical tests which used to cost a lot of time & money as well as cause unnecessary troubles. Mr Arabinda wanted to make the same quality of service, infrastructure & doctors of Kolkata available to the local peoples of Purba Burdhaman so that they can too live a better life.

With his decades of experience in his hand, he decided to give in his heart's desire & began the life long journey of serving others by providing quality diagnosis tests at an affordable cost. We can now proudly say that in our two decades of journey, we have successfully improved the quality of life of Kalna as well as Purba Burdhaman by serving more than One Million people :)
